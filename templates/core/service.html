Covid-RTPCR

An infection with SARS-CoV-2, or COVID-19, is detected using the COVID-19 test, which uses a technique called real-time reverse transcription-polymerase chain reaction (RT-PCR). Detects the genetic material of the virus extracted from a sample (usually a nasal swab or throat swab). The most accurate and reliable way to diagnose COVID-19 infection.

CBC

It provides information about blood cells such as Red Blood Cells (RBC), White Blood Cells (WBC) and platelets. A complete blood count is routinely performed to assess a patient's general health status. This test measures red blood cells, white blood cells, and platelets.

Learn More

CRP

The C-reactive protein (CRP) Test detects the presence of CRP in blood. CRP is a nonspecific marker of inflammation that can be triggered by infections, injuries, heart conditions, and autoimmune diseases. In addition to CRP, other tests are usually conducted to diagnose a particular condition and to determine the location of the infection and inflammation. They also help monitor the progress of treatment.

Learn More

T3, T4, TSH

A Thyroid Profile Total consists of a number of tests done together to detect or diagnose thyroid diseases. This test measures the levels of three hormones in the blood: Thyroid Stimulating Hormone (TSH), Thyroxine (T4) - Total, and Triiodothyronine (T3) - Total.

Learn More

HBA1c

A glycosylated hemoglobin test (HbA1c) measures how much sugar you have been consuming over the last 2-3 months. As part of health checkups, it is used to detect prediabetes, diagnose diabetes, and monitor the effectiveness of treatment. The sugar between the range  if < 5.7%, while values ≥ 6.5%  consider normal.

Learn More

LIVER FUNCTION TEST

This test measures the level of enzymes and proteins produced by the liver, such as bilirubin, albumin, lactate dehydrogenase, etc. Liver function tests (LFTs) are commonly used to assess the liver's function. LFT is advised in case of symptoms suggestive of liver problems such as dark urine, diarrhea, light stool, and jaundice. This test helps in diagnosing liver related conditions including liver cirrhosis, hepatitis, and liver damage for liver disorders

Covid-RTPCR

An infection with SARS-CoV-2, or COVID-19, is detected using the COVID-19 test, which uses a technique called real-time reverse transcription-polymerase chain reaction (RT-PCR). Detects the genetic material of the virus extracted from a sample (usually a nasal swab or throat swab). The most accurate and reliable way to diagnose COVID-19 infection.
